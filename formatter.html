Block, quote, bold, italic, underline: before:type:after will add the "before" and "after" around that kind of content.

Rule, newline: type:replace will turn it into whatever is after the ":".

Image template, link template: <img src=":img:" alt=":img_alt:" /> replaces the ":data:" parts with the relevant data, and uses that in place of the content.

Anything that's not one of these templates is ignored. Use \n to insert new lines.
